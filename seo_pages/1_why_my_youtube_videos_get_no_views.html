Why This Happens: The Brutal Truth About the Algorithm

YouTube is not a meritocracy. YouTube is a ruthless recommendation engine. Its goal—its entire business model—is to keep eyes on the screen to show ads. To achieve that, it tests your video with a small group. If they ignore it while scrolling, YouTube interprets that as "boring" and pulls the plug.

There are two non-negotiable metrics: Impressions and CTR. If your CTR is under 4%, it's a catastrophic failure in the eyes of the AI.
